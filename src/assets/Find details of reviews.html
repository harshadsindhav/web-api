Overview

Find the details of the reviews

Requires OAuth

No

Method

GET

URL

https://<hostname-api.sabacloud.com>/v1/review?q=(:criteria)&f=(:display)&count=:count&startPage=:startPage&includeDetails=:detailsFlag
